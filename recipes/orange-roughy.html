Baked Orange Roughy, Italian-Style

Description

Below is the ingredient list and recipe instructions for an Italian-style orange roughy courtesy of All Recipes

Ingredients

  • nonstick cooking spray
  • 1/4 cup Italian-seasoned bread crumbs
  • 2 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons grated romano cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoons salt, or to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 pound orange roughy fillets
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Coat a medium baking dish with nonstick spray.
  2. Mix bread crumbs, parmesan cheese, romano cheese, salt, and garlic powder together in a shallow bowl.
  3. Brush orange roughy fillets on both sides with melted butter, then dredge in the bread crumb mixture. Arrange fillets in a single layer in the prepared baking dish and sprinkle with parsley.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork, 10 to 15 minutes.
